What Is ETA?

Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition (ETA) is a path where you become a business owner not by starting a company, but by buying an existing small business with stable cash flow. Instead of building from zero, you step into something that already works and grow it from there. This allows you to still develop a similar skillset as a traditional entrepreneur without the high risk and uncertainty of a classic startup. Let's walk through the basics of how ETA works.

The ETA Process – Step by Step

Looking for an outline? Here are the main steps involved in acquiring and operating a small business today:

  1. Prepare Yourself as a Buyer
    • Learn valuation basics
    • Understand deal structures
    • Set personal investment criteria
  2. Source Potential Deals
    • Online business-for-sale marketplaces
    • Brokers
    • Direct outreach to business owners
  3. Perform Due Diligence
    • Financial review
    • Operational assessment
    • Risk identification
  4. Close the Deal & Begin Operations
    • Secure financing
    • Transition management
    • Improve and grow the business
